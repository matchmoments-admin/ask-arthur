05
CLONE & BRAND PROTECTION  ·  LIVE

Catching impersonation before victims do

Clone-watch
Daily sweep of newly-registered domains against a watchlist of Australian brands — surfaces look-alike domains the day they appear.
Certificate Transparency
Monitors public TLS-certificate logs (crt.sh) for certificates issued to brand-impersonating domains.
Shop Signal
Flags fake or high-risk online shops at scan time, with optional paid commerce-reputation enrichment.
Brand notification
Routes confirmed impersonations to each brand's security / fraud channel (security.txt, VDP, fraud inbox) for takedown.
Working in production: the first live brand notification (to a major Australian bank) was sent in May 2026. This is the platform's direct contribution to the SPF Prevent principle — see slide 10.

THE INTELLIGENCE ENGINE

A compounding view of the
Australian scam landscape

Feeds in
20+ automated scrapers — phishing & malware IOC feeds (URLhaus, OpenPhish, PhishTank, Spamhaus…), Australian regulator narratives (Scamwatch, ACSC, ASIC, AUSTRAC), Reddit scam discussion, and an inbound-email feed for threat newsletters.
Refined
PII scrubbed, entities linked, narratives extracted and clustered into themes (Reddit Intel, Sonnet 4.6 + Voyage embeddings), risk-scored — turning raw reports into structured, searchable intelligence.
Surfaced
Trending threats, emerging campaigns, impersonated brands and semantic search over the corpus — to consumers as warnings, and to partners through the B2B API.
The data moat: a uniquely Australian, real-time, community-fed scam corpus that gets more valuable with every check and every feed.

SCAMS PREVENTION FRAMEWORK

Built for the 1 July 2026 deadline

The SPF Act (Royal Assent Feb 2025) obliges 200+ banks, telcos and digital platforms to Prevent · Detect · Disrupt · Report scams — with penalties up to A$52.7M. Ask Arthur maps directly to these obligations.

Prevent
Certificate-Transparency & clone-watch alerts on brand-impersonating domains; real-time consumer warnings.
Detect
20+ feeds + community reports as external scam intelligence; domain aggregation surfaces emerging campaigns.
Disrupt
Actionable intelligence via API drives URL blocking and customer warnings; entity risk scores power decisions.
Report
Trending-threats endpoint + government-ready exports + onward reporting to regulators (potential AFCX feed).
Plus a free in-product SPF self-assessment (SPF / DMARC / DKIM email-auth health check) that any organisation can run today at askarthur.au.
