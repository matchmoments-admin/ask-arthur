## 3. Services

3.1. Provider shall make the Platform available to Client in accordance with this Agreement, the applicable Order Form, the SLA, and the DPA.

3.2. The Platform enables Authorised Users to submit suspicious content (text, URLs, images, phone numbers) for AI-powered scam analysis and receive structured risk verdicts (SAFE, SUSPICIOUS, or HIGH_RISK).

3.3. Provider may update the Platform from time to time to improve functionality, security, or performance. Material changes to functionality will be communicated with at least 30 days notice.

4.3. **Early Termination for Cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party:

- (a) commits a material breach and fails to cure within 30 days of written notice; or
- (b) becomes insolvent, enters administration, or ceases to carry on business.

4.4. **Termination for Convenience.** Client may terminate for convenience upon 60 days written notice, subject to payment of all fees through the end of the then-current term.
